A few words about me

I am a biologist by training; I hold a PhD in biochemistry. A couple of years ago I decided to step away from the bench lab work traditional for a biochemist.

Instead, I focused on genomics projects. I got to work with two interesting plant species — Amborella trichopoda, the earliest diverging species on the flowering plant tree of life; and the Water Lily, Nymphæa cærulea. I got to learn some coding skills on the job, and made good use of my free time to prepare for transitioning into a software engineering career.

My family currently lives in Benicia; prior to moving to California we lived for a number of years in Gainesville, Florida.

Things I love doing:

  • Observing plants and animals in their natural habitats
  • Hiking
  • Cooking
  • Watching tennis
